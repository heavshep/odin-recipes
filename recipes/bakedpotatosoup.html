Baked Potato Soup

Description

Thick and creamy. Uses leftover baked potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 12 slices of bacon
  • 2/3 cup of margarine
  • 2/3 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 7 cups milk
  • 4 large baked potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 4 green onions, chopped
  • 1 1/4 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  1. Place bacon in a large, deep skill. Cook over medium heat until browned. Drain, crumble, and set aside
  2. In a stock pot or Dutch over, melt the margarine over medium heat. Whisk in flour until smooth. Gradually stir in milk, whisking constantly until thickened. Stir in potatoes and onions. Bing to a boil, stirring frequently.
  3. Reduce heat, and simmer 10 minutes. Mix in bacon, cheese, sour cream, salt, and pepper. Continue cooking, stirring frequently, until cheese is melted.
