Spaghetti

Description

A quick and easy spaghetti recipe to serve the whole family.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 2 lb lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, minced
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 2 (10.75 ounce) cans condensed tomato soup
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salter water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  2. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook ground beef with onion until meat is brown. Drain. Return to meat mixture to pan with tomato paste, tomato soup, and water. Cook, stirring, until heated through and thickened, 5 to 15 minutes. Serve over cooked pasta
